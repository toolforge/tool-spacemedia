1. U Scorpii
This 3D model of such a nova outburst of an object called U Scorpii is based on a scientific simulation, informed by observations.

2. SN 1006
This 3D model of SN 1006 was created by constraining mathematical models of the object with X-ray observational data from Chandra to help explore how the clumping of material after the explosion and the acceleration of high-energy particles affects the structure of the remnant.
3D SN 1006 handout

Download:
3D files (BLAST Quarter Globe, EJECTA Quarter Globe, EJECTA Full Globe)
