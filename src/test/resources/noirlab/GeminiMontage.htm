Discovering Our Universe Together

NSF’s NOIRLab (NSF's National Optical-Infrared Astronomy Research Laboratory) is the US national center for ground-based, nighttime optical astronomy. The Association of Universities for Research in Astronomy, Inc. (AURA) operates these facilities and NSF’s NOIRLab under a cooperative agreement with the National Science Foundation (NSF).
