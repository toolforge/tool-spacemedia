Gen. Raymond Visits Vandenberg

U.S. Space Force Gen. John "Jay" Raymond, Chief of Space Operations, and U.S. Space Force Chief Master Sgt. Roger Towberman, Chief Master Sgt. of the Space Force, discusses the importance of space and the cruciality of the Guardians that make assured access to space possible on Oct. 11, 2022, at Vandenberg Space Force Base, Calif. Gen. Raymond spoke about these matters during an all-call located on base. (U.S. Space Force photo by Airman First Class Rocio Romo)
